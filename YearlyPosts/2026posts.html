ATLA finished

Date: 2026-05-16

So, I just finished Avatar: the last airbender animated series and it was amazing. Like genuienly one of the best animated shows of all time.

The characters, story and theme are all so good, literally everything comes together at the end. My favourite character is Sokka not only because of his rizz game but also because he is genuinly a great character overall who started out misogynistic and kinda annoying but later became to be a great leader who was humble enough to learn his mistakes. Uncle Iroh is my second favourite character because of his wisdom and love for Zuko. Okay so I knew Zuko would join the avatar gang soon but I didn't know that it would be so late. There was a time during the climax of season 2 where Zuko could've taken his uncle's advise and helped the avatar gang but he didn't and he betrayed his uncle. And at first I was getting kinda annoyed at first at Zuko but his later realization and redemption was so good because of these moments and I absolutely loved it.

The fights were also something that impressed me too because when I first heard about the powersystem it seemed too simple so I had low expectations but the fight choreography was genuinly so good at how it utilized these powers and the overall creativity of it all was great. I was kinda spoiled about the sub type bendings like metal and blood but I didn't know that it was from legend of Korra So I was kinda bummed out that there were not much of those but hey what I got was still good.

Overall, I am so glad that I watched this show and I absolutely loved it.

Chainsaw man ended

Date: 2026-03-24

I don't know how to feel about this. Chainsaw man is the manga that is closest to my heart. It has always impressed me, made me care about the characters, it also changed me. Part 1 was a fucking masterpiece. Part 2 started out strong and it went on to be ... confusing to say the least.

But I noticed the change in the artstyle, it has noticably regressed. but the core story and concepts were still interesting so I stuck around and then suddenly it just ended. I think Fujimoto (the author) just got tired of this story, it was obvious the first half of part 2 was very concise and focused but as the story went on the direction was everywhere but I thought it was delibrate and I didn't mind it. I think he just wanted to move on to the next story that he has cooked

The reaction of the community was hilarious asf tho, like they were coping so hard just like the stranger things fan coping that there will be secret episode. But they were over exagerating. The ending is very confusing for me and it took multiple analysis videos to understand it. when I was reading it I genuinly was having fun but it just didn't seem real, It didn't seem to be fujimoto's style at all. The ending is NOT bad, I think the message is good but I feel like it wasn't fleshed out enough and we didn't get proper resolution to other characters and plot points.

But I do hope Fujimoto will continue this story a year or two from now. There is just so much potential.
