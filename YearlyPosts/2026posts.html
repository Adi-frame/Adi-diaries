Internet ban

Date: 08-04-2026

The internet is banned for 3 days... suposedly. It is now the second day. baning internet usage has become a regular occurance nowadays in my state. They always say its going to be for 3 days or 5 days only but it always gets extended. The cause is the ongoing Meitei and Kuki conflict that is happening, and the hate messages on social media. I bet they are doing this just to hide their incompetence. They think if they do nothing the problem will go away, and while that is true for small twitter and reddit conflict, it will never work in this case as it involves people losing their lives.

I couldn't even care less the reason for this particular ban. I am tired of this. Thankfully I stored some movies on my laptop and phone. From now I will not delete movies I have watched on laptop.

So, today I watched fight club. Unfortunately, I was not able to store this movie on my laptop but at least I watched it.

Oh and Chutki (our cat) gave birth to three kitten yesterday, and my sister was so anxious about it that it's hilarious. Man I cannot believe Chutki became a mother, I still remember the day she was born and how she was spoiled so much by her mother Tara.

Chainsaw man ended

Date: 24-03-26

I don't know how to feel about this. Chainsaw man is the manga that is closest to my heart. It has always impressed me, made me care about the characters, it also changed me. Part 1 was a fucking masterpiece. Part 2 started out strong and it went on to be ... confusing to say the least.

But I noticed the change in the artstyle, it has noticably regressed. but the core story and concepts were still interesting so I stuck around and then suddenly it just ended. I think Fujimoto (the author) just got tired of this story, it was obvious the first half of part 2 was very concise and focused but as the story went on the direction was everywhere but I thought it was delibrate and I didn't mind it. I think he just wanted to move on to the next story that he has cooked

The reaction of the community was hilarious asf tho, like they were coping so hard just like the stranger things fan coping that there will be secret episode. But they were over exagerating. The ending is very confusing for me and it took multiple analysis videos to understand it. when I was reading it I genuinly was having fun but it just didn't seem real, It didn't seem to be fujimoto's style at all. The ending is NOT bad, I think the message is good but I feel like it wasn't fleshed out enough and we didn't get proper resolution to other characters and plot points.

But I do hope Fujimoto will continue this story a year or two from now. There is just so much potential.

I joined a quiz competition

Date: 24-01-26

I still can't believe it... but I won the competition!

So you see there was a quiz competition happening at Navodaya vidyalaya Kakching, Manipur and I was asked to joined the competition. Even though I wanted to decline it so bad, the teachers were looking at me with such anticipation and expectation that I failed to do so properly (I said I did not want to do it).

So I looked at the bright side and thought "I am going to have a new experience!". The teacher said it was going to be a team competition So, I decided to shift my responsibility to others which I am not proud of but she was kind and smart. I want to be somewhat more responsible next time.

Ultimately, the day of the competition came and I thought I was the most unprepared out of the three students from our school but the competition turned out to be a MCQ and So, I gambled and... I somehow won the first prize! LIKE BRO WHAT!? I admit I knew about 60% of the questions but some where pure guess (Also the quiz was a inter-school competition). I didn't know how to feel about this I felt very undeserving of this and also I thought of huge expectation the teachers would place on me now.

But I won I guess and the prize was 1500 rupees. Also the quiz was just a part of a program to raise awareness of operation sindoor.
