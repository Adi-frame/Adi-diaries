Chainsaw Man

Chainsaw Man is my no. 1 favourite anime/manga. It was also one of my first manga and I thankfully went in blind. Some people judge it by the fanservice but the series is much deeper than that. It's about loss, family, purpose, and identity. Brilliant characters, crazy action, amazing story.

Attack on Titan

I couldn't watch it earlier because I was broke, so I was spoiled a lot of the story, But it still hit me hard. Great lore, characters, and one of the best stories ever. Season 4 took it to another level. Some minor nitpicks about the ending, but overall it's a classic.

One Piece

I regret sleeping on One Piece. Although, the beginning part of the anime was average with a little charm at best it kept getting better and better. Skypiea is where it hooked me — the lore, worldbuilding, and character dynamics are insane. I haven't completed it yet, but it's already in my top 3.

Vinland Saga

“I have no enemies.” This moment defines the series. Incredible character development and powerful themes. Nearly every major character is written with depth. A fantastic story overall.

Mob Psycho 100

Mob is overpowered but doesn't let that define him. He focuses on improving himself in other ways. Motivational show, great characters, 3 seasons of clean animation, and a perfect ending.

Death Note

This was one of my early anime I watched and oh boy I had never seen anything like this, since it was my first thriller-suspence media, and I fell in love wiht this anime. The plot was so interesting, the characters were so smart and charming. It is one of the GOAT of anime

Jojo's Bizarre Adventure

This is one of the most unique or should I say bizzare anime I've seen in my life. One of the greatest power system, lovable casts and unique storyline.

A silent voice

This is a very beautiful movie about redemption and forgiveness. I really like the journey of the main character and it is also very inspiring.

Perfect Blue

Mind bending anime. This will make you question what is real and what is fake with great suspense and mystery.

Takopi's Original Sin

What the fuck. This is one hell of a rollercoaster ride. This will make you experience all kinds of emotion and overall very heartbreaking and heartfelt anime.
